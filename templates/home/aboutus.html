About Green Basket

At Green Basket, we are dedicated to providing fresh, organic produce straight from local farms to your doorstep. Our mission is to promote healthy living and support sustainable farming practices.

Our Story

Green Basket was founded with the belief that everyone deserves access to fresh, organic food. We started our journey in 2023 with a small network of local farmers, and today, we deliver to thousands of homes across the region.
